the stovetop

Until a few months ago, I'd never eaten pasta in my Asian household -- rarely do we cook outside Chinese cuisine. Motivated by the closures of seated dining after March, I got to learning recipes for foods I'd only ever had when eating out. Here are some of the recipes I've loved, and hope you do too:

  • Japanese milk bread
  • Kombucha
  • Curry udon
  • Cinnamon rolls
  • Kimchi
