the wall

Mildly embarrassing, and very personal, I have a wall of quotes in my room. These are my words to live by -- they're not revolutionary, but they ground me. They bring me peace, and maybe it can for you, too.

"Make the chili." (from here)

"Juggling is knowing your glass and plastic balls." (paraphased from here)

"Live against your deathbed regrets." (inspired by this)

"Your lows define your highs."

"I shall pass this way but once; any good that I can do or any kindness I can show to any human being; let me do it now. Let me not defer nor neglect it, for I shall not pass this way again."

"Every man I meet is my master in some point, and in that I learn of him."

"Think big picture."

"All is temporary."

"Don't regret anything you do, because in the end it makes you who you are."
